Values queue

This panel appears only when the values module is on (SETUP.md). It holds employers your rubric could not clear or exclude outright, phrased as a 30-second question for whoever you escalate to. Define your floor in research\HALAL-RUBRIC-TEMPLATE.md; answers get cached in memory\shaykh-rulings.md. One fictional example below.

Q1Consultancies whose clients include interest-core banks (FICTIONAL EXAMPLE)
SituationThe firm builds software and advises many clients, some of them conventional banks. My workData and optimization on non-bank projects, but the employer can re-staff me. QuestionIs employment there permitted while my own projects stay clear? Does it change if I can decline bank-client assignments in writing?

Cached rulings in force

SR-001Aggregate-logistics case · FICTIONAL EXAMPLE
RulingPlanning logistics where some shipments may contain an excluded product is permitted when the planner cannot know and cannot check which shipments contain it. BoundaryKnowledge is the hinge: identifiable excluded lines in your own work make it impermissible; genuine blindness within a permitted-core employer does not.

How your machine works

You: say Go in the Job Hunter Claude session (any day, any model)
↓
It reads your words first. Corrections, journal commits, preferences, values answers: everything you saved in this app or wrote in inputs\
↓
Hunts. Fresh vacancies from the mapped sources, filtered by YOUR preferences, deduped against memory
↓
Filters, hard. The values floor and your rulings first (if the module is on), then language, hours, commute, the traineeship cap
↓
Packages the best. Tailored CV and letter (every line traced to documented evidence), an interactive page, a 3-minute quick-verify
↓
Two blocking auditors. Honesty (no claim without proof) and values (no doubt packaged) can each kill any package
↓
You press every send button. The machine never submits, never emails, never posts. Ever.
↓ ↑
Your feedback loops back. Every commit becomes a numbered rule; every rejection a short hypothesis; acceptance rate is tracked honestly
It grows sideways. Weekly portfolio sync (GitHub + your folders) · monthly Project Forge (first Friday, defined together) · skills radar every run

Everything is files you can read: CLAUDE.md (the law) · memory\ (what it remembers) · briefs\ (what it did) · FAILURE-MODES.md (what could go wrong and who catches it) · GUIDE · SETUP
