# Standing rules

Loaded at every gate. Max 3 edits per run, each citing its origin (a C-number from corrections-log or a board minute). Invariants live in CLAUDE.md, not here. ID note: R-nnn here are behavior rules; values rulings are SR-nnn in memory\shaykh-rulings.md (do not confuse the namespaces).

Seed your own rules from your corrections over time. The two below are sensible starting defaults you can keep, edit, or delete:

- R-001 | Warm routes first: always leverage people you know; packages name a findable human; internal or referral routes outrank cold queues. | Origin: default seed
- R-002 | Commute exception: base cap <your city> + <N> min; a longer commute is allowed when the role is strong and hybrid (2 to 3 office days) is plausible; the package states the honest arrangement. | Origin: default seed
