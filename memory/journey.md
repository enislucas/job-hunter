# Journey log

The full life of every application, one section per id, one dated line per event, newest last (chronological within a section). You report anything in free words (corrections inbox or a page's feedback commit); the gate step parses it into events here and keeps `memory\applications.md`'s status column as the coarse summary.

Stage vocabulary (flexible, grow it as reality demands; any free-form stage is allowed):
`prepared, accepted-suggestion (you approved the package in the app; only then does it appear in the Journal panel), rejected-suggestion (you declined the package; move ledger to withdrawn, log the reason as a correction), submitted, acknowledged, knockout-passed, online-assessment, recruiter-call, interview-hm (hiring manager), interview-case, interview-onsite, interview-panel, reference-check, offer, negotiation, JOB (accepted and started), rejected-<stage>, ghosted (no reply 3+ weeks after a stage), withdrawn, expired (posting died)`.

Rules: on any `rejected-*` or `ghosted`, the next run writes a REASONS block, maximum 5 lines, hypothesis not autopsy, plus at most one lesson candidate for LESSONS.md if a pattern repeats. On `offer` and beyond, everything moves to "Needs your call" priority in the brief. On `JOB`: keep the section open and log key activities you perform, because that log becomes the first draft of your next CV.
