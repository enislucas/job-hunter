## SR-001 (FICTIONAL EXAMPLE, delete on first real run), aggregate-logistics case

Planning logistics where some shipments may contain an excluded product is permitted when the planner cannot know and cannot check which shipments contain it. Generalizes only to roles with the same structure: aggregate planning, no item-level knowledge, no ability to inquire.

Boundary: knowledge is the hinge. When the excluded goods are identifiable to the person in the role (item-level visibility, named excluded lines in the data), the work is impermissible; when the flow is genuinely anonymized and unknowable, this permission applies.
